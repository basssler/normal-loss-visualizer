How to read it

This section stays educational and clean, without changing the rest of the app’s structure.

Student cues
Move toward the valley: the lowest region of the landscape suggests better Q and R combinations.
Service rises with R: increasing the reorder point usually improves service but can raise holding-related cost.
Q has tradeoffs too: very small Q drives more ordering cost, while very large Q raises carrying cost.
Prototype scope: this section only • ready to drop into an existing inventory learning app
